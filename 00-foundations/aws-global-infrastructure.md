# AWS Global Infrastructure

Understanding Regions, Availability Zones, Edge Locations, and how they affect architecture decisions.

## Key Topics

- **Regions** — geographic areas with 2+ AZs; how to choose a region (latency, compliance, service availability, cost)
- **Availability Zones** — isolated data centers within a region; designing for AZ failure with Multi-AZ deployments
- **Edge Locations & Regional Edge Caches** — CloudFront PoPs for content delivery and Route 53 latency routing
- **AWS Local Zones & Wavelength Zones** — extending AWS to metro areas and telecom networks for ultra-low latency
- **Global Services vs. Regional Services** — IAM, Route 53, CloudFront are global; most others are regional
