# AWS Solutions Architect Associate (SAA-C03) — Study Notes

The core SA certification — tests your ability to design resilient, cost-optimised, and secure AWS solutions.

## Exam Overview

- **Code:** SAA-C03
- **Duration:** 130 minutes
- **Questions:** 65 (scored, mix of single/multiple response)
- **Passing Score:** 720/1000
- **Domains:**
  1. Design Secure Architectures (30%)
  2. Design Resilient Architectures (26%)
  3. Design High-Performing Architectures (24%)
  4. Design Cost-Optimised Architectures (20%)

## Domain Notes

### Design Secure Architectures
- <!-- IAM least privilege, cross-account roles, SCPs -->
- <!-- S3 bucket policies, OAC, pre-signed URLs -->
- <!-- VPC security: SGs, NACLs, VPC Flow Logs -->

### Design Resilient Architectures
- <!-- Multi-AZ vs. Multi-Region -->
- <!-- DR strategies: Backup/Restore, Pilot Light, Warm Standby, Active-Active -->
- <!-- Auto Scaling groups, ELB health checks -->

### Design High-Performing Architectures
- <!-- Caching: CloudFront, ElastiCache -->
- <!-- Storage: right-sizing EBS, EFS throughput modes, S3 Transfer Acceleration -->
- <!-- Database: Aurora read replicas, DynamoDB DAX, RDS read replicas -->

### Design Cost-Optimised Architectures
- <!-- EC2 purchasing options: Spot for batch, Reserved for steady state, On-Demand for spiky -->
- <!-- S3 lifecycle policies and storage class transitions -->
- <!-- Serverless where it makes cost sense (Lambda vs. always-on EC2) -->
